## How to sign

When You open Your first pull request, the **CLA Assistant** bot will comment on the PR with a link to sign this Agreement. Following that link will take You to [cla-assistant.io](https://cla-assistant.io), where You will authenticate with GitHub and confirm Your acceptance. Once signed, the CLA status check on Your pull request will turn green and the signature will cover all of Your future Contributions to the Project.

If You wish to withdraw consent or have Your signature record removed, please open an issue on this repository or contact the Project Maintainers directly. Note that withdrawal does not retroactively revoke the licenses granted under Sections 2 and 3 for Contributions already merged.
